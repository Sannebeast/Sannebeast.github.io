Why HBO-ICT?

The most important question to ask myself is why? Why HBO? Why ICT? Why am I here on the HZ?
But what is my motivation behind everything? What's my motivation to study HBO-ICT on HZ in Middelburg? To find out what my intrinsic motivation is, I've to ask myself lots of why's. So let's start with it!

Why did I choose for ICT?
I choose for ICT because I always liked play games and
I was curious about how the pc works and why.

Why do I want to know about pc's?
I want to know about pc's because
I want to understand what I'm working with

Why do I want to understand working with pc's?
It makes me feel less helpless when
a simple bug pops on the screen

Why don't I want to feel helpless?
I don't want to feel helpless because
I like to have everything in my control.
It makes me comfortable knowing I have control over the situation.
Generally I am just curious about how a pc works.
